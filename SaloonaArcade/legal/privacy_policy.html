Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2025

Saloona Arcade respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the limited data we collect from you. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information Collected

  • Wallet Addresses: When you connect to DexHunt, we store your public Solana wallet address to enable gameplay and track rewards.
  • Gameplay Metrics: Score history, NFT ownership, and win/loss records may be stored to facilitate competition and reward delivery.
  • Local Storage: We may use browser-based local storage to maintain session state and display features such as jackpot status.

All data is processed solely to deliver core game functionality and rewards. This is done under the legal basis of user consent and legitimate interest in operating a fair and secure skill-based platform.

2. How We Use This Information

  • Enable in-game functionality, such as wallet-restricted rewards
  • Display leaderboard and jackpot status
  • Provide NFT claim verification
  • Prevent fraudulent gameplay

We retain gameplay data only as long as necessary for operational purposes. All browser-side and server interactions are secured using current encryption standards.

3. Personal Data & Identification

We do not collect any personal data such as names, addresses, or emails. We only interact with public blockchain identifiers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your data. Some public data such as wallet addresses and game stats may be visible on-chain or on leaderboard pages.

If you believe any of your game-related data should be removed, you may submit a request through our contact page or email us at privacy@saloona.gg. We will honor valid requests to the extent allowed by law and technical feasibility.

5. Third-Party Services

DexHunt and Saloona Arcade may use external APIs (e.g., Phantom Wallet, Solana blockchain) to verify transactions and gameplay outcomes. These services have their own privacy policies.

6. Your Consent

By using the game and connecting your wallet, you consent to this privacy policy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected here with updated effective dates.

If you have concerns, do not connect your wallet or use the game until you're comfortable with this policy.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under data protection laws, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your data. Contact us at privacy@saloona.gg to exercise these rights.
