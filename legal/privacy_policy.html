Saloona Arcade is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect data related to your use of DexHunt and the Saloona Arcade platform, a Web3 gaming service on Solana. By using the platform, you consent to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to enable gameplay, reward distribution, and platform functionality. During beta testing on Solana Devnet, all data is processed for testing purposes and has no real-world implications.

  • Public Wallet Addresses: When you connect a Solana wallet (e.g., Phantom Wallet), we collect your public wallet address to facilitate gameplay, track simulated rewards ($SALOONA, NFTs), and enable swaps via Jupiter.
  • Gameplay Metrics: We store data such as score history, reaction times, win/loss records, and NFT ownership to support leaderboards, competitions, and reward verification.
  • Local Storage: We use browser-based local storage to maintain session state, display features like jackpot status, and enhance user experience.
  • Analytics (Optional): We may use anonymized analytics to improve gameplay, such as tracking game performance or user interactions, with your consent where required.

No personally identifiable information (e.g., names, emails, addresses) is collected. All data is processed under the legal basis of user consent and our legitimate interest in operating a secure, skill-based gaming platform.

3. Data Storage and Security

We store data securely using industry-standard encryption (e.g., HTTPS, secure APIs) for server and browser interactions. Public wallet addresses and gameplay metrics are stored on secure servers or, where applicable, on the Solana blockchain (Devnet during beta).

Data Retention: Gameplay data is retained only as long as necessary for beta testing or until you request deletion. After beta testing concludes, data may be anonymized or deleted unless required for legal compliance.

Blockchain Transparency: Public wallet addresses and on-chain actions (e.g., $SALOONA swaps, NFT claims) are inherently visible on the Solana blockchain. This is a feature of blockchain technology and not within our control.

4. Personal Data and Identification

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, or physical addresses. Our interactions are limited to public blockchain identifiers (e.g., Solana wallet addresses) and gameplay metrics.

During beta testing on Solana Devnet, no real-world personal or financial data is collected or processed.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your data to third parties. However, certain data may be publicly visible:
